About Me

Violin

I've played the violin for 7 years (and the piano for 6). My favorite piece is Jean-Baptiste Accolay's Concerto No. 1 in A Minor (see Perlman's wonderful performance here). I've even written a few (very short) compositions of my own and performed in a friend's short film! Music has always been my sanctuary, allowing me to express myself freely and connect with others.

Chess

Chess is a hobby I've picked up more recently. I started playing at the start of the pandemic in 2020. Back then, I was only rated 600 elo. After playing countles games and solving a seemingly endless number of puzzles, I'm proud to say I've climbed the ranks since then. I'm now rated 1800 on Lichess and 1700 on Chess.com. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to play a game!

Taekwondo

My go-to conversation starter is always that I have a 3rd degree black belt in Taekwondo, a pursuit that I've poured well over a decade of my life into. I credit this lifelong journey with teaching me discipline and perseverence. Beyond personal achievements, I also really enjoy teaching newcomers to the sport. I volunteer to teach at my local studio every Saturday.
